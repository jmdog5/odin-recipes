Description

This New York-Style Cheesecake recipe yields a classic, creamy cheesecake with a velvety texture and a subtle tang from the cream cheese. Baked in a buttery graham cracker crust and lightly flavored with vanilla, itâ€™s a rich, indulgent dessert that can be enjoyed plain or with your favorite fruit topping.

Ingredients

  • Graham cracker crumbs
  • Melted butter
  • Sour cream
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cream cheese
  • White sugar
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Lemon zest
  • Orange zest

Steps

  1. Make the crust and press it into a prepared springform pan.
  2. Make the filling and pour it over the crust.
  3. Bake until the edges are puffed and the center is slightly jiggly.
  4. Let the cheesecake cool in the oven.
