Split Pea Soup

Description

Split Pea Soup is a hearty and comforting dish made from dried split peas simmered with vegetables like onions, carrots, and celery. Often flavored with smoky ham or bacon, this thick soup is rich in flavor and nutrients, making it a perfect meal for chilly days. Itâ€™s easy to prepare, requiring simple ingredients that come together to create a wholesome and satisfying bowl of warmth.

Ingredients

  • Dried split peas
  • Cold water
  • Ham bone
  • Vegetables: 2 onions, 3 carrots, 3 celery stalks, 1 potato
  • Spices and seasonings: salt, black pepper, dried marjoram

Steps

  1. Soak, drain, and rinse the split peas. Place them in a pot.
  2. Add the water, ham bone, onions, and seasonings to the pot.
  3. Bring to a boil, then simmer for about 90 minutes.
  4. Remove the meat from the ham bone and return the meat to the pot
  5. Add the vegetables and cook until the vegetables are tender.
