New York Style Cheesecake

Home

Description

New York-style cheesecake is a rich, dense, and creamy dessert made with a simple combination of cream cheese, eggs, sugar, and a hint of vanilla, typically baked in a graham cracker crust. It's known for its smooth, velvety texture and slightly tangy flavor, often served plain or with a topping of fresh fruit or sauce.

Ingredients

  • Graham cracker crumbs
  • Melted butter
  • Sour cream
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cream cheese
  • White sugar
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Lemon zest
  • Orange zest

Steps

  1. Make the crust and press it into a prepared springform pan.
  2. Make the filling and pour it over the crust.
  3. Bake until the edges are puffed and the center is slightly jiggly.
  4. Let the cheesecake cool in the oven.
