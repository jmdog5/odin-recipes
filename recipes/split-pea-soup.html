Split Pea Soup

Home

Description

This Split Pea Soup recipe offers a hearty and nourishing bowl filled with tender split peas, carrots, celery, and onions, simmered to perfection. Flavored with smoky ham or bacon, the soup is both comforting and rich, making it a satisfying choice for a cozy meal on a chilly day.

Ingredients

  • Dried split peas
  • Cold water
  • Ham bone
  • Vegetables: 2 onions, 3 carrots, 3 celery stalks, 1 potato
  • Spices and seasonings: salt, black pepper, dried marjoram

Steps

  1. Soak, drain, and rinse the split peas. Place them in a pot.
  2. Add the water, ham bone, onions, and seasonings to the pot.
  3. Bring to a boil, then simmer for about 90 minutes.
  4. Remove the meat from the ham bone and return the meat to the pot
  5. Add the vegetables and cook until the vegetables are tender.
